FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does TL;DR work?
TL;DR uses advanced AI to analyze the content of any webpage you visit. Simply click the extension icon, and within seconds you'll get a concise 3-bullet summary of the key points. It works on articles, blog posts, news stories, documentation, and more.
Is my data private?
Absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your browsing data and the content of the pages you summarize are never stored on our servers. All processing happens either locally in your browser or through encrypted connections. We don't sell data, we don't track you, and we don't show ads.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time with no questions asked. After canceling, you'll continue to have Pro access until the end of your current billing period, then automatically switch back to the Free plan. There are no cancellation fees or hidden charges.
